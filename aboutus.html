About Us

We established NSB Naturals in 2019, with a vision to make quality products available to the customers at affordable prices. We make this possible by sourcing directly from our network of farms supplying fruits, vegetables and groceries. The scale of operation allows us to offer unparalleled freshness.

Our fleet and logistics including integrated cold-storage vehicles where appropriate, help ensure freshness of the produce, minimise waste, and reduce carbon footprint. We also have a dedicated R&D team with over 2 years of experience in Organic and other specialist agricultural practices.
